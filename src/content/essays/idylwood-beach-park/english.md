Idylwood Beach Park is a cute little park located on the banks of Lake Sammamish. It features a children's play area, a designated swimming area for kids, a sandy beach, and several other interesting spots for entertainment.

The park is home to several ducks, who often walk gracefully in a flock and capture the attention of visitors. It's a beautiful sight to see them silently swimming in the lake.

There's also a floating platform on the lake that allows visitors to stand and appreciate the beauty of the water. The gentle rocking of the platform creates a pleasant experience for those who take advantage of it.

Walking on the sandy beach is also a great experience, and kids can play with beach toys, build sandcastles, and dig pits. The play area features swings, slides, and monkey bars for children to enjoy, while there's also a handball court and a walking trail in the park. Additionally, the community center hall is a popular location for birthday celebrations.

One unique feature of the park is the stream of water that flows into the lake. There are several small bridges built on the water streams, including a hidden bridge at the beginning of the stream that offers a great view of the water gushing at high speed. 

During the summer months, visitors can relax on the lawn and soak up the sun or play games like flying disc and Frisbee. Boats can also be hired during this time. Occasionally, food stalls are set up, and the park is popular with Redmond residents.

Many people also enjoy fishing here, with several species of fish, including perch, smallmouth bass, largemouth bass, and salmon, found in the lake.

Finally, some visitors enjoy playing loud music and singing along when they take motor boats out on the lake. Overall, Idylwood Beach Park is a great destination for people looking for a fun and relaxing day out.
